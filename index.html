Hello, I’m Min, an experienced UX researcher having a background in Psychology / Business(B.A.) and Human Computer Interaction(M.S.). I’m currently working as a user experience researcher at SK planet, Korea.

To make people’s lives happier and easier with usable technologies, I conduct design research and discover meaningful findings. My primary goal is conducting and probing UX research across mixed methods(both quantitative and qualitative) to turn into actionable insights at any design phases that help the service move forward.

With an open mind, I stay curious and enjoy to expmeriment with new technologies with the eyes of user centered design.

My journey so far, I've had many learnings from failures and successes. I’m still learning every day and pushing myself beyond boundaries.

WORKS & SKILLS

WORKS

Making 'Driving Pattern' more informative based on analyzing usage data and usability testing
To improve user engagement of ‘Driving Pattern’ providing behavioral analytics on driver and benefit of insurance, analyzed usage data and conducted the rapid user studies. After updates, the number of active users increased over 300%.
Field research in India to discover the needs of prepaid users
Truebalance is the effective app to manage mobile balance having 60 millions users in India. In the early design phases, I contributed to discover the needs of pre-paid users and conducted user research in remote/field, and iterative usablity testing in evolving key structures of design.
Developing methods for visual design competitive usability tests
Created research methods to measure how visual design of the mobile OS affects the overall UX. After conducting experts/user interviews in Korea and U.S. and synthesizing all results, defined the research method inclusively measuring the competitive landscape of visual design usability.
Discovering the needs of Hi-Resolution music lovers in Japan
To create design strategies of hi-resolution music service in Japan, deliberately designed a survey to measure five motivational aspects of listening to music before recruiting participants. Based on analyzing survey data, diary studies and interviews, delivered a design concept all backed up evidence.
Designing and researching the online advertising interaction model in Social Media based on Psychological theories
To improve the user experience of social media advertising, created the novel interaction design model based on psychological theories. Conducted A/B between subject design experiments to measure usability compared to current interface.
Above are some of projects I enjoyed working on, all works and detailed information are available to request. Feel free to contact me!
